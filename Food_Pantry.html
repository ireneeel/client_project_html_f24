University of Michigan Food Pantry

Welcome to the U-M Food Pantry

Our mission is to provide students in need with access to nutritious food and resources that support their well-being. We are dedicated to ensuring that no student goes hungry while pursuing their education at the University of Michigan.

Our Services

The U-M Food Pantry offers a range of services for students facing food insecurity:

  • Weekly distributions of pantry staples and fresh produce
  • Meal kits for students in emergency situations
  • Nutrition guidance and meal planning resources
  • Special workshops on food budgeting and healthy eating
