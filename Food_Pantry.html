University of Michigan Food Pantry

The University of Michigan Food Pantry is dedicated to supporting students facing food insecurity by providing access to nutritious and balanced meals. Our mission is to ensure that no student has to choose between their education and their next meal.

Our Services

We offer a variety of services to help students, including:

  • Weekly food distributions
  • Fresh produce and pantry staples
  • On-campus pickup locations
  • Nutrition workshops and resources

How to Access the Pantry

Students can access the Food Pantry by filling out a simple registration form. After registering, you will receive further details about available pickup times and locations.
